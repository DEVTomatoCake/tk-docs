title: Giveaways
lang: en
---

To manage giveaways you need the "Manage Messages" permission.

## Create

`giveaway start`

## Edit

`giveaway edit <message ID> <Time> <Winners> <Prize>`

## Reroll winners

`giveaway reroll <message ID>`

## End

`giveway end <message ID>`

## Delete

`giveway delete <message ID>`

## List giveaways

`giveway list`
