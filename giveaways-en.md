---
title: Giveaways
lang: en
---

To manage giveaways you need the "Manage Messages" permission.

## Create

`giveaway start <Time> <Winners> <Prize>`

## Edit

`giveaway edit <Nachrichten-ID> <Time> <Winners> <Prize>`

## Reroll winners

`giveaway reroll <Nachrichten-ID>`

## Delete

`giveway delete <Nachrichten-ID>`
